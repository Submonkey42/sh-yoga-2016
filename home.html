Shasta Yoga Class


For most of us, stress is unfortunately a daily part of our lives. However, with this restorative but strengthening practice you can leave feeling blissfully calm and relieved of the stress and anxiety you may feel in your daily life. Our practice combines gentle stretching and restorative techniques with poses that challenge your endurance. Paired with mindful breathing and a serene guided visualization, this makes up a sequence that will benefit both your body and your mind. Overall, this practice will allow you to improve your strength and balance as well as be centered, focused, relaxed, and refreshed as you face the rest of your day.


Yoga and Mindfulness

Us yogis believe that all people should give yoga a try and practice mindfulness. Yoga is more than just doing the poses on a mat. It is a lifestyle. When one does yoga, yoga finds its way to seep into aspects of their life. Yoga also builds mindfulness. within us. Mindfulness to us is developing the ability to objectively observe our thoughts and feelings both mentally. It means being aware of our surroundings and sensations, and it allows us to become present instead of allowing life to pass us by.
